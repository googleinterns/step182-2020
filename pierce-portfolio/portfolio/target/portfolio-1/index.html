Pierce D. Wilson


Hey Team! My name is Pierce Wilson and I'm a '23 at Dartmouth College. I plan to complete a double major in Computer Science & Geography, along with a minor in Public Policy.

Outside of class this year, I wrote new The Dartmouth, America's oldest college newspaper, did research in the African & African American studies department, and organized with the fossil fuel divestment campaign. I am really interested in social impact and the nonprofit sector, so I also did some volunteering.

This is my first 'real' job, but I've held a few different positions in the past. At school this year, I worked as an administrative assistant at the business school, a communications intern in the sustainability office, a notetaker in the Computer Science department. In high school, I worked at the Boys & Girls Club, TJMaxx, and A Better Chance (educational access nonprofit).

In my free time, you can probably find me riding my bike, watching tv, baking, or brewing my own homemade kombucha. Feel free to ask me about any of these things!
